About Us

Codemate Club was born in December 2022 out of a collective desire among NEHU students to create a space where coding enthusiasts could collaborate, compete, and learn together. What began as small group discussions and problem-solving sessions soon evolved into a structured club, bringing students across disciplines under one roof to nurture innovation and technical growth.

Our Vision

To foster a strong, collaborative, and innovative coding community in NEHU and beyond.

Our Mission
